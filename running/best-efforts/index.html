New App Available - Strafforts

If you would like to help out testing the new app, please head over to strafforts.yizeng.me to play around, where you can connect with your own Strava account and see your estimated best efforts data.

It's only just work in progress, so it's not open sourced yet. The Heroku server only starts when someone visits the app, which means the new app will take a while to load if it's been inactive for more than 30 minutes.
